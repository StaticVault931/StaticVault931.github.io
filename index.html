Discover Movies, TV Shows & Anime on StaticVault931

StaticVault931 is a free discovery platform for movies, TV shows, and anime. Browse trending titles, all-time classics, hidden gems, and personalized recommendations tailored to your taste — no account or sign-up required. Every title includes ratings from IMDb, Rotten Tomatoes, and Metacritic, full cast and crew details, trailers, and streaming availability across Netflix, Disney+, Prime Video, Max, Hulu, and more.

Personalized For You

Tell us your favorite genres and titles, and your home feed adapts — from Korean thrillers to feel-good comedies to hard sci-fi. The Clips feed lets you scroll trailers like shorts to discover your next binge.

Deep Search & Filters

Search by title, actor, director, topic, or keyword — with typo tolerance, year detection, and filters for genre, decade, country of origin, runtime, rating, and streaming service. Find hidden gems the algorithms skip.

Track Everything

Build a watchlist, mark titles watched, like what you love, and pick up where you left off with Continue Watching — all saved locally, private to you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is StaticVault931 free?

Yes — browsing, discovery, personalization, and tracking are completely free, with no account required.

Where does the data come from?

Title data comes from TMDB, anime data from AniList, and ratings from IMDb, Rotten Tomatoes, and Metacritic via OMDb. StaticVault931 does not host any media.

Can I see where a movie is streaming?

Yes — every title page shows current streaming availability across major services, including free options.

Does it work on phones and tablets?

Yes — the entire site is built for phones, tablets, laptops, and TVs, with a mobile navigation bar and touch-friendly controls.
